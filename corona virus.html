WHAT IS SYMPTOMS

Symptoms of the Corona virus
Headache, cough, muscle pain, difficulty breathing,
high temperature and stress
The symptoms of the Corona virus begin with fever
then dry cough, and after a week it leads to shortness of breath
and some patients need treatment in the hospital

METHOD OF PREVENTION

Prevention of the Corona virus
Wash hands with soap and water,
cover the nose and mouth by bending the elbow or
with a tissue when coughing or sneezing
and disposing of the tissue immediately Rub hands with an alcohol disinfectant after every
direct contact Stay at a distance of a meter or more from anyone
who is coughing or sneezing Clean your hands
before wearing the muzzle, and before and after taking it off
Make sure it covers your nose, mouth, and chin.
When you take off the muzzle, store it in a clean plastic bag,
or dispose of it in a waste bin if it is a medical mask.

TREATMENT OF CORONAVIRUS

Until now, the competent authorities have approved only one drug to treat Covid 19.
There is currently no medicine available that fully cures Covid 19.
Antibiotics are ineffective against viral infections, such as Covid 19.
Researchers are currently testing a variety of potential treatments. Cushions relieve symptoms, and may include: Pain relievers (ibuprofen, acetaminophen) Cough syrup or medicine Comforts Drink fluids
